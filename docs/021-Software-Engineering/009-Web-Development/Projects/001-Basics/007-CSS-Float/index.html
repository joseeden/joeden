Convicted Murderer - Extremely Dangerous

Sirius Black, formerly a trusted ally of the wizarding world, was convicted for the mass murder of twelve Muggles and one wizard, Peter Pettigrew, in broad daylight with a single curse.

A known supporter of the Dark Lord, Black was also implicated in betraying James and Lily Potter to Lord Voldemort, leading to their tragic deaths.

Black escaped from Azkaban Prison, making him the first known wizard to break free from its high-security confinement. His whereabouts remain unknown, and he is considered armed and highly dangerous. Approach with extreme caution and do not attempt to engage.

If spotted, contact the Ministry of Magic immediately. A substantial reward is offered for any information leading to his capture.
