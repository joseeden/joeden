About Us

Founded in 2009, Circuit Minds is a forward-thinking electronics engineering company specializing in design automation and cutting-edge technology solutions. Our team of expert engineers leverages the latest advancements in automation to streamline complex electronic design processes. We are committed to innovation, ensuring that our clients receive high-quality, efficient, and future-ready engineering solutions.

Our Mission

At Circuit Minds, we are a team of engineers and developers who are passionate about electronics and technology. We believe that the future of electronics lies in the hands of those who can harness the power of advanced algorithms to build smarter, faster, and more efficient electronics. Our mission is to help engineers around the world build the next generation of electronics that will power a sustainable future for all.

Services

Harnessing the latest advancements in machining, we offer a wide range of in-house processes and work with high-performance materials, from steels and carbides to aerospace-grade alloys.

From AI-powered thermostats to autonomous vacuum robots, our smart home solutions enhance convenience, security, and energy efficiency with intelligent automation.

We provide industrial controllers, power supplies, logic boards, and displays that power critical operations across manufacturing and automation industries.

Our avionics systems include precision sensors, high-resolution displays, and advanced flight control systems, ensuring safety and reliability in aerospace applications.

We develop power generation and distribution systems, automation controls, and digital solutions for industries such as healthcare, transportation, and smart cities.

Our product range includes PLCs, servomotors, CNC machines, HMIs, inverters, industrial robots, transformers, and software solutions to optimize modern industrial operations.
